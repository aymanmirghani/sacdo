# Privacy Policy

**Sudanese American Community Development Organization (SACDO)**
**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 18, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

The Sudanese American Community Development Organization ("SACDO", "we", "our", or "us") operates the SACDO mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By registering or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide
- **Full name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number**
- **Membership type and status**

### b. Payment Information
We use **Stripe** to process membership payments. We do not store your full card number, CVV, or banking details on our servers. Stripe may collect and store payment information in accordance with their own privacy policy.

### c. Device Information
- **Push notification token** — used to send you invoice and membership notifications
- **Biometric data** — if you enable biometric login (Face ID / fingerprint), this is processed entirely on your device by your operating system. We never access or store your biometric data.

### d. Usage Data
- Invoice history and payment records associated with your membership
- App activity logs for administrative and audit purposes

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your membership account
- Generate and send monthly membership invoices
- Process membership payments via Stripe
- Send push notifications about invoices and organization updates
- Allow administrators to manage membership records and audit actions
- Respond to your inquiries and provide support
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** We use Firebase (Google) for data storage and authentication, Stripe for payment processing, and SendGrid for email delivery. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
- **Organization administrators:** Authorized SACDO administrators can view your membership information, invoices, and payment records within the App.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of SACDO and its members.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using **Google Firebase (Firestore)**, hosted in the United States. We implement access controls so that members can only access their own data, and only authorized administrators can access member records.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your membership account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations. If you wish to have your account and data deleted, please contact us at the address below.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Request deletion** of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
- **Opt out** of push notifications at any time through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 10.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The SACDO App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
